Itinerary Highlights

Day 1

Arrival & Souq Waqif

Arrival at Hamad International Airport. Late afternoon visit to the traditional Souq Waqif for dinner and shopping.

Day 2

City Wonders & Museum Visit

Tour of the Corniche and the iconic Museum of Islamic Art. Afternoon visit to the futuristic Katara Cultural Village.

Day 3

The Pearl & Desert Escape

Explore the man-made island 'The Pearl-Qatar'. Evening thrilling desert safari to Khor Al Adaid (Inland Sea).

Day 4

Leisure & Departure

Free morning for Lusail Boulevard. Afternoon transfer to airport for departure.
