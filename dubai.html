Ultimate Luxuries & Desert Adventures

5 Days 4 Nights Luxury Dubai Tour

Experience the best of Dubai with our all-inclusive luxury package. From the heights of Burj Khalifa to the depths of the Arabian desert. This package is perfect for first-time visitors and families looking for a mix of adventure and comfort.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Dhow Cruise Dinner

Arrive at Dubai International Airport. Meet our representative and transfer to hotel. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Dhow Cruise with a buffet dinner and live entertainment on Dubai Creek.

Day 2

Dubai City Tour & Burj Khalifa

Modern Dubai tour including Palm Jumeirah (one-way Monorail), Burj Al Arab (photo stop), and Jumeirah Mosque. Afternoon visit to the 124th floor of Burj Khalifa for panoramic views.

Day 3

Desert Safari Adventure

Morning at leisure. Afternoon 4x4 Desert Safari with dune bashing, camel riding, sandboarding, henna painting, and a delicious BBQ dinner with belly dance & Tanura show.

Day 4

Miracle Garden & Global Village

Visit the world's largest natural flower garden - Dubai Miracle Garden. Later, head to Global Village for cultural experiences, shopping, and international food (Seasonal).

Day 5

Leisure & Departure

Breakfast at the hotel. Free time for last-minute shopping at Gold Souk or Dubai Mall. Transfer to the airport for your flight back home.

Travel Tips:

Dubai is a modern city but respects local traditions. Dress modestly in public areas and always carry a reusable water bottle to stay hydrated!
