Truly Asia: Malaysia

Nature, Culture & Modern Wonders

6 Days 5 Nights Wonders of Malaysia

A perfect combination of Kuala Lumpur's city lights and the tranquility of Genting Highlands. This package offers a deep dive into Malay culture and world-class attractions.

Key Features:

  • Kuala Lumpur City Tour
  • Genting Cable Car Ride
  • Batu Caves Exploration
  • Premium Transportation
  • Hotel Breakfasts Included

Travel Tips:

Pack light cotton clothes for KL but bring a light sweater for Genting Highlands as it gets chilly up there!

Itinerary at a Glance

Day 1

KL Arrival & Check-in

Transfer from KLIA to your city hotel. Evening free to explore Bukit Bintang shopping area.

Day 2

City Tour & Batu Caves

Visit Petronas Twin Towers, King's Palace, and the famous rainbow steps of Batu Caves.

Day 3

Genting Highlands Full Day

Day trip to Genting. Enjoy the Awana SkyWay and the indoor/outdoor theme parks.

Day 4-5

Malacca Historical Tour

Optional tour to Malacca city - a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its history and food.

Day 6

Last Look & Departure

Breakfast at hotel. Final shopping trip for souvenirs before airport drop-off.
