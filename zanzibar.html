Zanzibar: The Spice Island

Exotic Beaches & Endless Wonders

5 Days 4 Nights Zanzibar Beach & Spice Retreat

Escape to the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean. This package offers a blend of relaxation on white sandy beaches and cultural immersion in the historic Stone Town.

What's Included:

  • 4-Star Beach Front Resort
  • Daily Breakfast & Dinner
  • Stone Town Guided Tour
  • Spice Plantation Exploration
  • Roundtrip Airport Transfers

Why Zanzibar?

Known for its rich history, unique wildlife (Red Colobus monkeys), and world-class diving sites. A perfect romantic or family getaway.

Your Journey

Day 1

Arrival & Stone Town

Meet and greet at the airport. Transfer to Stone Town. Evening walk through the narrow alleyways and Forodhani Gardens food market.

Day 2

Spice Tour & Beach Transfer

Morning Spice Plantation tour. Experience why Zanzibar is called the 'Spice Island'. Afternoon transfer to Nungwi beach resort.

Day 3

Prison Island & Dolphins

Boat trip to Prison Island to see Giant Tortoises. Optional snorkeling stop to spot dolphins in their natural habitat.

Day 4

Leisure Day

A full day to relax by the pool or enjoy the pristine white sands of Nungwi. Optional sunset Dhow cruise in the evening.

Day 5

Departure

Breakfast at resort. Enjoy one last dip in the ocean before transfer to the airport for your journey home.

Paradise Awaits

Escape to the beautiful island of Zanzibar.
